About

Team up with one or more friends and cooperate or compete with each other to make your city the worlds greatest. You can also play by yourself. Build and manage your city placing resident zones, making sure there is enough power and that the people can get to their jobs by building roads.

You can race with your friend to win by making the biggest or most populated city in a set number of years or you can take it easy and just play together helping each other make one great city.

The game places you in position of mayor of a city or part of a city. Everyone can enjoy this game. Play in your own pace with a colleague at work or race with one of your class mates.

Develop

If you want to help develop Simulty you can take a look at the project wiki or our sf.net page where you can find more information. Another option is to send an e-mail to simulty@tigge.org.

Simulty is free software (GPL v2) and is designed to be a cross platform product that works on at least Windows, Linux and Mac and hopefully everywhere else where the libraries we use can function.

Currently Simulty uses Allegro and is known to compile and work (with the cmake system) on at Windows and Linux, but should also work on Mac.
